Services

Our Services

FoVET is a development-oriented organization covering research and consultancy services providing baseline surveys, mid and endline evaluations, needs assessments, & reviews, strategic planning & policy formulation, research (qualitative and quantitative), youth & social participation, capacity building and gender research & analysis.

Research

We offer high quality research with experienced personnel in both qualitative and quantitative research. Our comparative advantage is qualitative research with personel capable of producing high quality analytical reports. For quantitative research, we have contacts across the whole of Kenya and have networks with organisations that can reach other parts of Africa. Our team has experience in both virtual and in-person data collection methods using technology. We have experience in baseline surveys, midline and endline evaluations, needs assessments, context analysis, user experience research and other researches.

Consultation

Focused on helping individuals and organizations towards their journey of deploying Best Practices for Excellence, FoVET works with individuals and organizations mainly in mostly not-for-profit sector to support their monitoring, evaluation research and learning skills and knowledge. Our services aid organisations to tap more into the data that they have to make decisions that improve their strategic, adaptive and sustainable programming. We carefully select our consultants in order to offer our customers the foremost quality of expertise together with extensive experience in industry practices. Our consultants have a substantial track record of executing major consulting assignments involving organization-wide deployment, multi-geography, and on advisory mode for continuous process improvement.

Training

Our training is focused on being responsive to individual and client needs. The training approach is based more on practical applications and theory is used to emphasize practical and real life experiences. Story telling and experience sharing is a key component of our training and we encourage consultants and trainees to tell stories of their experiences and get lessons from them. We conduct both virtual and in-person trainings on issues related to research methods, learner assessments, strategic programming, qualitative research, survey methods and general monitoring & evaluation. We also are planning to introduce courses on financial management, visioning and goal setting, and personal strategic management for individuals. Being a learning organization, FoVET thrives on feedback & customer satisfaction score, and provide inputs in the solution development program
